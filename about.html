A Little About Us:
The Copper Moon was founded in May 2011. The owner, James Orlando, who has always been infatuated by the beautiful craft of being a barista, came to downtown Cleveland to share with you his fresh, tasty and elegantly brewed drinks.

Coming from the baking legacy of the Orlando, James carries on the high standard when it comes to his crafts. You can expect every item you get at the Copper Moon is made from scratch, with the utmost attention to detail and the pride of a barista veteran.
Our Offerings:
The Copper Moon offers a huge selection of 250 drinks made from freshly ground coffee beans, fruits and ice cream. A new, unique drink is usually created every month.
Our Achivements:
For four consecutive years, The Copper Moon has been chosen as the Café of the Year by the Downtown Cleveland Residence Association (2013-2017).

The shop has eperienced 25% annually compouned growth and 87% customer return rate.
